## 1. Information the Application collects and uses

### 1.1 Information you provide

When you register and use the Application, you may provide:

- **Account data:** email address and password (the password is handled securely on our servers; we do not store it in plain text for display in the app).
- **Profile and family data:** name, phone number (when requested), role in the family (e.g. administrator or collaborator), and information about your household/family as needed for the service (tasks, assignments, points, achievements, rewards, and related features).

This information is used to **create and manage your account**, **provide the core features** of the Application, and **maintain security** of the service.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the Application, certain technical information is processed in the normal operation of the service, including:

- **Network and server logs:** when the Application communicates with our **API over the internet**, our servers may process data such as your **IP address**, timestamps, and request metadata. This is standard for hosting and security and is **not** used to provide GPS-based or “map” location features inside the app.
- **Device context:** the **operating system** type/version may be implied by the app platform (Android/iOS). The Application **does not** integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the current version (no Firebase Analytics, AdMob, etc., in the published codebase).

The Application **does not** request access to your device’s **precise or approximate GPS location** and **does not** collect geolocation for personalization, recommendations, or analytics as described in generic templates.

The Application **does not** use artificial intelligence (AI) inside the client app to process your personal data for features.

### 1.3 Local storage on your device

The Application may store **session-related data** (for example access tokens or preferences) on your device using the platform’s storage mechanisms (e.g. **SharedPreferences** on Android) so you can stay signed in and use the app efficiently.
